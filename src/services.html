How to Read This Table

Each tier builds on the previous one. Higher tiers do not replace features, instead expanding the scope, depth, and long-term maintainability.

Starter

Ideal for portfolios, landing pages, and small static sites. Focused on clean presentation, responsiveness, and essential accessibility.

Professional

Designed for small businesses and growing projects. Adds structured development, improved accessibility, optional backend integration, and a more collaborative workflow.

Enterprise

Intended for platforms, products, or long-term projects. Includes scalable architecture, advanced APIs, full accessibility audits, and extended post-launch support.
